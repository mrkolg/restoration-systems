Our Vision

RS seeks to improve the quality of environmental restoration in order to expand the opportunities for compensatory mitigation as a business and a public policy tool.

We hope to increase the appreciation and utilization of compensatory mitigation by policymakers, regulators, developers and environmentalists; and we do so by locating the best restoration properties, performing superior projects, and communicating our success in the following years. Our work is not done until all environmental impacts result in appropriate, cost-effective and timely mitigation off-sets.

Our Approach

From our start in 1998, Restoration Systemsâ€™ approach to compensatory mitigation has been to locate the very best restoration and protection opportunities and develop an enduring relationship with the property owner based on mutual trust and respect, based on doing what we said we would do, when we said we would do it.

When land rights purchased, Restoration Systems draws on its deep experience and the specific skills of carefully chosen technical providers to present conservative, defensible and attractive plans for the restoration and protection of the property as mitigation.

When presenting our plans to government authorities and seeking permits to do our work, we treat our public sector regulators with respect and understanding, keeping in mind the public trust they represent. Above all, RS shall build good mitigation-- at a profit if we can, at a loss if we must. But always good mitigation.
